About Me

I'm Gloria, a UX/UI and web designer currently based in Madrid. With 7 years of experience in digital design — the last two focused on UX/UI design — my goal is to create digital experiences that meaningfully connect users with brands.

My journey into UX design started with web design in CMS platforms like WordPress and Drupal. Over time, my curiosity led me to question every design decision, focusing on understanding why we design the way we do and how we can make it truly human-centered.

My experience in web design and digital marketing has taught me to find the balance between usability and aesthetics, always designing with empathy and a clear understanding of the audience. For this reason, in my daily work, I strive to bridge corporate goals with user needs by designing intuitive interaction flows, accessible information architectures, and visually engaging designs.

I am currently completing a master’s degree in UX/UI Design, Web Design with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, and Web Development at CEI: Centro de Estudios de Innovación. I am always exploring new ideas, especially those related to psychology and artificial intelligence tools that help me better understand users and open new possibilities in design.
